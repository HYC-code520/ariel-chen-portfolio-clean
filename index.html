About me

Hello, I’m Ariel. I design and build digital products with a strong focus on frontend experience, usability, and visual storytelling. My work is influenced by fashion and visual design, especially proportion, detail, and consistency, but what drives me most is how design makes people feel calm, confident, and supported.

I came to New York through fashion, where I learned that great work is not only beautiful, it is considerate. I treat branding and visual identity as part of the experience from day one, then implement the system in code so it stays cohesive and real. I enjoy working across the full flow, from UI design and frontend build to backend integration and deployment.
